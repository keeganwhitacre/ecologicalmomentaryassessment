Cardiac Interoception

Audio Setup

This task requires precision timing to match a sound with your heartbeat.

⚠️ Crucial: Do not use Bluetooth headphones.

Wireless headphones (like AirPods) introduce an audio delay that invalidates the data.

Please use wired earphones or your phone's built-in external speaker in a quiet room.

Getting ready

Place the tip of your index finger gently over both the camera and flashlight on the back of your phone.

Keep your finger perfectly still — even small movements disrupt the reading.

The flashlight will turn on automatically to detect your pulse.

Baseline complete ✓

In the main task, you'll hear a series of tones. Each tone represents one of your heartbeats — but it may be artificially delayed or early.

Your job is to drag the slider until the tone feels perfectly in sync with your actual heartbeat.

Important

Do not actively try to feel your pulse with your other hand. We are measuring your natural internal perception.

Keep your finger steadily on the camera throughout the entire trial block.

You'll start with 2 practice trials.
